[myOKR](https://github.com/trongdth/myOKR) is a cross-platform desktop app
for productivity and goal management. I've spent the last three months
living inside it - building features, then testing them on my own goals
every single day. Version **0.2.0** bundles everything that survived.

You can [download 0.2.0 here](https://github.com/trongdth/myOKR/releases/tag/v0.2.0).
Here's what's inside.

---

## ☀️ Daily Focus

- **Today View** - curates your daily tasks based on active Key Results
  and your Pomodoro budget. Open the app, see exactly what today is for.
- **First-Run Walkthrough** - onboarding slides introduce the core app
  loop the first time you open the app.

## 🎯 Goal Management (OKRs)

- **OKR Tree** - create monthly cycles, define Objectives, and break them
  down into measurable Key Results.
- **Cycle Cloning** - seed a new cycle from the previous structure, and
  delete empty cycles cleanly.
- **Weekly Review Wizard** - guides you through reflections with progress
  charts and auto-populated focus data.

If you're setting up your first cycle, I wrote a full guide on
[how to design effective OKRs with myOKR](https://code4food.work/blog/effective-okrs-with-myokr/) -
three life zones, small Key Results, and a reward ritual that keeps the
system alive past month three.

## 🔁 Habit Tracking

- **Calendar Grid** - log completions daily at a glance on an interactive
  calendar grid.
- **Streaks** - current and best completion streaks are calculated
  automatically.
- **Goal Integration** - link habits to Key Results so your OKR progress
  updates itself.

## 🍅 Productivity Tools

- **Pomodoro Timer** - focus and break cycles with system tray integration
  and notifications.
- **Generative Focus Music** - live-synthesized ambient music, fully
  offline, via the Web Audio API.
- **Eisenhower Matrix** - prioritize tasks into four quadrants with inline
  editing and drag-to-reorder.
- **Markdown Notes** - tasks and descriptions support full GitHub-flavored
  markdown.

## 🔒 Desktop & Sync Experience

- **Local-First Storage** - your data lives locally in a secure Automerge
  document. No account required.
- **Dropbox Sync** - seamless cross-device sync using your own Dropbox
  account.
- **Minimize to Tray** - runs quietly in the background with native system
  tray icons.
